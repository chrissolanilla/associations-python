Welcome to the Connections Widget Creator

What is connections?

Connections is a game that involves the player selecting a group of words and checking if all the words in their selection are connected in some type of way. The goal of the player is to correctly match the words with all the groups before they run out of lives or attempts.

As the creator, you can customize aspects of the game such as how many lives the player has, how many groups of words are in the game, and how many words for each group.

To get started, please enter a game name and grid size.

You can change both of these at any time later on.
