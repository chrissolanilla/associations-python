Welcome to the Associations Widget Creator

Associations is a game that tasks students with matching words and phrases based on a common topic or theme.

Customize your Associations game by selecting a grid size and selecting a theme or topic for each category. The topics should be distinct from one another. Then, populate each card within every category with words or phrases associated with the topic.

Enter a name for your widget and select a grid size. You can modify both options later.
